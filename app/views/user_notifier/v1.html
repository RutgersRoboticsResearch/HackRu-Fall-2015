Known as R-Cubed by its members, we are the club to help you pursue robotics. The mission of R-Cubed is to grow the robotics scene at Rutgers. We are dedicated to educating new members on the construction and application of autonomous robots, with special emphasis given to teaching "SLAM", which stands for Simultaneous Localization and Mapping. Through weekly meetings, social networking, and the Rutgers Robotics Hackathon, R-Cubed seeks to foster student involvement in both personal and group robotics projects. R-Cubed aims to help bridge members with the vast resources Rutgers has to offer by fostering relationships with faculty, USACS, IEEE, Hackrspace, and Maker Space. R-Cubed looks to pair motivated members with cutting edge research projects, funding, and like minded roboticists, engineers, computer scientists, and hackers.
